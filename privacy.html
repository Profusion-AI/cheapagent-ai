Privacy, in plain language

Last updated 2026-06-10 · applies to CheapAgent v0.2 beta

Your documents stay in your browser

When you paste or upload a document, the conversion runs locally in your browser using the open-source doc2toon engine. The text of your document is never uploaded, never stored, and never sent to a server or an LLM API. This is true whether you are signed in or not.

What we store if you sign in

Signing in raises your daily allowance from 1,000 characters per run to 15,000 characters per day. To enforce that limit we keep a minimal account record:

  • Your user id and email address (managed by Netlify Identity).
  • How many characters and conversions you have used today.
  • Timestamps for when the account was created and last used.

That is the whole list. No document bodies, no document titles, no conversion history. When your browser checks your remaining allowance, it sends only a character count — a number — not the text itself. Counters from previous days are discarded; we keep only today's totals.

What we never store

  • The contents of anything you paste or upload.
  • Your converted TOON output.
  • Passwords — credentials are handled entirely by Netlify Identity, not by CheapAgent code.

Anonymous use

You can use CheapAgent without an account. Anonymous use makes no network requests with your content or its size; the 1,000-character limit is applied in your browser.

Cookies and local storage

CheapAgent uses browser local storage for two things: your light/dark theme preference, and your Netlify Identity session token if you sign in. There are no advertising or analytics cookies.

Third parties

CheapAgent is hosted on Netlify, and sign-in is provided by Netlify Identity. Netlify processes requests as our hosting provider; see Netlify's privacy policy for how they handle infrastructure-level data such as IP addresses in server logs.

No billing in v0.2

The v0.2 beta has no paid tier and collects no payment information.

Questions or deletion requests

To ask a question or have your account record deleted, open an issue at github.com/Profusion-AI/cheapagent-ai or contact the maintainer listed there. Because we store so little, deletion means removing your Identity account and the usage counter keyed to it.

Changes

If this page changes in a way that affects what is stored, the change will be noted in the project changelog with the date.
